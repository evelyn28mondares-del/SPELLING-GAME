Word to Word Match

Player 1 (Left Side)

Score: 0
Time: 30s

Player 2 (Right Side)

Score: 0
Time: 30s

Controls:

Mouse: Drag items from the right column and drop them onto the matching words on the left column.

Keyboard Player 1: Use W/S to navigate right side, Space to select, A/D to jump to left side, W/S to choose target, Enter to drop.

Keyboard Player 2: Use Up/Down Arrows to navigate right side, Right Ctrl to select, Left/Right Arrows to jump to left side, Up/Down Arrows to choose target, Enter to drop.
